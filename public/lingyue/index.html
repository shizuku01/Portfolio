One brief, twelve designs.

Every page below is live — and you can restyle any of them yourself. No design experience needed.

Shift + E
Open the editor On any design below. Or click the “Edit design” button in the bottom-left corner.

Nothing here is public — changes only affect what you see, and Reset puts everything back.

Colour tab
Ready-made palettesOne click recolours the whole page. Start here.
Main coloursThe four that matter: accent, background, text, lines.
More coloursEvery remaining colour, folded away until you want it.
+ Save currentKeep a palette you like and reuse it later.
Text tab
Font pairingsSwap the whole typeface set at once. Missing fonts load automatically.
Text sizeOne slider scales every heading and paragraph together.
FontsSet headings, body and labels separately. Preview updates live.
Use fonts from my computerAdds your installed fonts to the list. Chrome/Edge only — otherwise just type a font name.
Fine tuningBody size, line spacing and letter spacing, if you want to go further.
Layout tab
Add a sectionDrag a block from the panel onto the page. A green line shows where it lands.
Move or deleteHover any section — a small toolbar appears with up, down and delete.
Page widthMake the content column wider or narrower.
ZoomScale the entire page to check how it feels denser or roomier.
Edit just one section
“Editing” dropdownSwitch from Whole page to a single section. Everything then applies to that one only.
Outline buttonBoxes every section. Hover to highlight green, click to select it.
Save tab
Copy the CSSCopies your colours and fonts as code, to paste into the page and keep them.
Saved looksName a look, then load it back any time.
Download / Open fileMove a look to another browser or send it to someone.
Undo · Redo · ResetAlways at the bottom of the panel. Ctrl+Z works too.
